Game Title: Match To Bake

Matching cards easy, doing it while serving customers, no one told me that!!!

Genre

Puzzle Sim

Platform

Desktop only

Story

You have been left with a restaurant and need to serve some customers, problem being you don't know how to cook. Luckily your grandpa thought of this when he left you the restaurant and has given you magical cards that will make the food for you. Now the only problem is to manage this during rush hour.

Esthetics

Graphics: Realistic, well as Realistic as pngs of random people and food will take me

Sound: Like animal crossing in the sense the people will speak gibberish and the music will be like cafe music. There will be a click when you selected a card and sounds if you complete or miss an order.

Gameplay

Mechanics

The player can clcik on the cards to serve the food and can activate a slowdown to help players when they are getting stressed out.

Control

Mouse and Keyboard.

Teaching the Game

There will be an instruction screen and then that's it for learning the game.

Player Learning

Players will need to navigate the board and remember card placements to successfully pass through the level.

About the development of the experience

This project did not turn out the way i wanted, and i can only blame myself for the lack of effort regarding it. The game as display above was suppose to be a quick paced puzzle game that was supposed to challenge the user to try to keep up with the orders given out by the customers. I definitely did not give myself enough time to work on the project and make it as I foresaw it as. The code is not working as the cards the customers want do not interact with the cards that you own currently making it just a game that doesn't work. For the project itself I tried make just the action of picking the customer's card the priority, but I couldn't get the interaction to work out. SO I do have the board cards and the Above cards in there respective places, but just no interaction between them.

For the things I do have I tried to make it look good and pleasing. The cards are well even out with good designs for both the front and back of the cards. The table where the cards you own is well space to fit like a pop up shop stall and when the player interacts with the cards and audio clip is played to let the player know of the interaction. At all times some background Music is playing to give the player a sense of belonging in the scene. The HTML and CSS is valid and the code conventions follow what was asked I hope. I made an ES6 class for the cards themselves to allow for ease of use.
