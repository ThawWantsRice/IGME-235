Process

So yeah I wanted to make a website that would allow a user, someone who likes dogs hopefully, to search up random images they could view. I also realized that for the site to be any useful i would have to save the images the user likes and keep them saved until they want to view them. I then realized I also would have to give them the option to delete the images they have saved. So that was the idea. I wanted to have a home button, but couldn't figure out font-awesome.
